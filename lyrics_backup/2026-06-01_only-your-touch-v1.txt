[Hook]
Come a little closer to the firelight glow
I just want to memorize the way you move so slow
There is nothing left to hide when the shadows fall
Only your touch can make sense of it all

[Verse 1]
Midnight falls across this quiet little town
We are the only souls awake for miles around
Your fingers trace a gentle story on my skin
And I am ready to let this night begin

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is pulling me closer to the edge
Trading all my secrets in the warmth of our bed
I can feel the rhythm of your racing heart
Lighting up the dark

[Chorus]
Only your touch can set my soul on fire
Burning through the chill with a quiet desire
You are the whiskey warm that I want to taste
Losing all my senses in our secret space

(Only your touch, every breath I take)
